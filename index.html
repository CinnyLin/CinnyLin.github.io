About

I started coding rather late, I only learned Python until second year in college. Even then I couldn't really understand what I can do with code, until volunteering at an educational platform working on logistics automation and data analysis. I am often drawn to aesthetic graphic designs, which is probably why I enjoy plotting data, and why I started learning web development.

I worked on and oversaw a lot of consulting projects in freshman year in college. The summer before sophomore year, I interned at a unicorn tech startup in Jerusalem and successfully supported the launch of its product in Taiwan. Currently, I am leading the MasterMind program for the digital portal of an accelerator in Berlin that promotes female entrepreneurship. I enjoy working cross-functionally and I am passionate about projects that bring real social impacts.

I once had the anxiety of taking up on leadership positions, for fear that I didn't deserve the position because my team members was more talented than me. Now I understand that what is more important as a leader is to know how to delegate tasks, facilitate discussions, and bring positivity to the team.

I love visiting new places and learning different cultures. The opportunity to live in different cities has taught me to be adaptable and made me a fast learner.
